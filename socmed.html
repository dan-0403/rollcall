Social Media Policy — RollCall Attendance Management System

Effective date:

Purpose Scope Our Social Media Use User Guidelines Brand Usage Privacy Reporting Third-Party Disclaimer Policy Updates

1. Purpose of the Policy

This policy outlines how the RollCall Attendance Management System manages its official social media presence and how users may interact with our content. This policy ensures respectful engagement, preserves data privacy, and protects RollCall’s brand reputation.

2. Scope of This Policy

This policy applies to:

  • Users interacting with RollCall on social media platforms.
  • Students, employees, administrators, or organizations using our services.
  • Individuals or entities referencing RollCall publicly online.

3. Our Official Social Media Use

RollCall may use social media to:

  • Post updates, announcements, and educational content.
  • Promote features, releases, and collaborations.
  • Offer help resources and customer support guidance.

RollCall will never request passwords, attendance data, verification tokens, or sensitive personal info via social media.

4. User Interaction Guidelines

Users agree not to:

  • Post unlawful, abusive, defamatory, discriminatory, or obscene content.
  • Share confidential student or employee attendance data.
  • Promote false claims or impersonate RollCall.
  • Spam or advertise unrelated content.
  • Harass RollCall users, staff, or partners.

RollCall may delete content that violates these rules and block or report users when necessary.

5. Use of the RollCall Brand

Users may share screenshots, reviews, or content containing RollCall solely if such use:

  • Does not misrepresent the RollCall system.
  • Does not violate copyright, data privacy, or intellectual property laws.
  • Does not expose sensitive educational or organizational data.

Commercial use of RollCall’s logo or brand requires written permission.

6. Privacy & Data Protection

RollCall will not disclose private attendance records through social media. Support requests requiring personal or educational data will be redirected to secure communication channels.

RollCall handles all data according to its official Privacy Policy.

7. Reporting Misuse

Users may report impersonation, harassment, copyright breaches, or abusive content through:

Email: Rollcall@gmail.com

RollCall may report illegal conduct to authorities when necessary.

8. Third-Party Platforms Disclaimer

Interactions with RollCall through third-party platforms are governed by their own policies and may involve data tracking. RollCall is not responsible for misuse, security issues, or data policies outside our control.

9. Updates to This Policy

RollCall may revise this policy periodically. Continued engagement after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
