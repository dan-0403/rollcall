RollCall System Tutorial

Learn how to use RollCall as an Admin or Employee with this complete guide.

Administrator Tutorial

Guide for managing the system, employee accounts, and attendance records.

1. Log In as Administrator

Use your admin credentials to sign in and access the dashboard. This is where you see the system overview.

2. Understanding the Dashboard

The dashboard displays:

  • Total employees
  • Employees on time today
  • Late employees
  • On-time percentage

3. Viewing Attendance Records

The Attendance table shows daily logs including:

  • Date
  • Employee ID
  • Name
  • Status (On Time / Late / Absent)
  • Time In & Time Out

4. Adding Attendance Manually

Click + Add New to create a new attendance record. Fill in:

  • Employee ID
  • Name
  • Time In
  • Time Out
  • Status

5. Managing Employees

Go to the Employees tab where you can:

  • Add new employee accounts
  • Edit employee info
  • Delete employee profiles

6. Viewing Attendance Logs

Check the Attendance Logs section to view raw time-in and time-out submissions.

7. Generating Reports

Use the Sheet Report panel to export attendance reports (daily, weekly, monthly) as spreadsheets.

Employee Tutorial

Guide for regular employees using RollCall for attendance.

1. Log In as Employee

Enter your credentials to access your personal attendance page.

2. Time In

On your dashboard, click the Time In button to record your arrival. The system will save the exact timestamp automatically.

3. Time Out

When leaving, press the Time Out button. This updates:

  • Your daily log
  • Your attendance status
  • Your total working hours

4. Viewing Your Attendance

You can view your attendance history including:

  • Dates present
  • Time In & Time Out
  • Lateness or on-time status
  • Absences

5. Notifications & Alerts

Employees receive notifications for:

  • Late marks
  • Schedule changes
  • Admin announcements
  • Attendance corrections

6. Updating Your Profile

Some systems allow employees to update personal info (email, contact, password). If enabled, find it under Profile.

7. Logging Out

Click Logout after your shift to keep your account secure.
